Our Services

  • FOR HOME
  • FOR BUSINESS
01

General Pest Control

Prokil Pest apply the latest & most effective techniques including spraying of approved chemicals, traps/trays, application of gel baits and other form of baiting systems. We choose the products poses minimal risk and special care and attention are taken to minimize any possible adverse impact to the environment.

02

Termite

Many termite species can do significant damage to unprotected buildings and other wooden structures. Their habit of remaining concealed often results in their presence being undetected until the timbers are severely damaged. We at Prokil Pest provide an effective termite treatment to rid of these pest, elevating your worries on your properties and assets.

03

Fogging

Fogging is a technique used for killing insects that involves using a fine pesticide spray which is directed by a blower. Our team at Prokil Pest are equipt and ready to provide fogging services at a moments notice.

04

Pre-Construction Soil Treatment

Pre – Construction Soil Treatment is commonly know as treating the initial construction area for termites and other pest be for the slab is poured onto it. The preventative cost of a Pre-Construction Treatment is minimal in comparison to the monetary damages termites can cause a home after it is built.

05

Fumigation

Fumigation is recognized as an effective means of targeted control of Stored Product Insects (SPIs) such as beetles, weevils, mites and moths. It can safeguard businesses such as shipping and cargo, warehousing and agriculture. We provide all forms of fumigation which includes for Palm Kernel Expeller (PKE), Wood Products and Wooden Pallets and Vessels or Ships.

06

General Pest Control

We also provide extensive General Pest Control services for warehouses, factories and offices in a commercial capacity. Just as our residential service, we will evaluate your pest condition and suggest a solution best to overcome it.

07

Disinfection Service

Disinfection is important for keeping properties and the people in them healthy and safety. There are many types of sanitation tools and equipment in the market. Our surface sanitisation solution aims to provide customers a peace of mind. In addition, assurance in terms of safety, efficacy and credibility are our key priorities too.
